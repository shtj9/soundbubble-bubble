New Release

Stiff Buscemi

Formed in 2018, Stiff Buscemi is an instrumental psychedelic rock band from Novi Sad, Serbia. Inspired by the music of the Balkans, the Middle East and North Africa and highlighted by wah guitars.

Their debut album Jet-Black Highdooks stands out among today's oriental rock in its meticulous production, rich sound, and playful compositions that shift between energetic, unpredictable moments to atmospheric passages.
